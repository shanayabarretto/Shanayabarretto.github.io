Extracurricular Activities

Design Teams

I've harboured an interest in space for a long time, and begged my parents to take me to the Kennedy Space Center in 2019, which was my favourite trip of all time. Thus, when I got to Waterloo, I had to join some space related teams. Currently I'm a member of Waterloo Orbital and Waterloo Rocketry.

On Orbital I do more electrical/circuit related work. I learned how to properly source components, how to use Altium, and a lot about MOSFETs and load switch circuits which was what I worked on for the Electrical Power Systems team this past term.

I did more mechanical related work on Rocketry. Through contributing to the Rogallo Wing design, I gained practice doing stress tests on SolidWorks and expanded my knowledge of design principles.

Engineering Ambassadors

I became an engineering ambassador this past term and had a lot of fun connecting with prospective students and answering their questions. I remember attending open houses and using university resources like EngChats last year when I had a never ending list of questions, and was so happy to get well thought out answers from those ambassadors, that I wanted to be on the other side of those chats this year, and help some younger students out. I often meet with students over video chats to answer all their questions, and ran a chat booth this year during the Waterloo open house.

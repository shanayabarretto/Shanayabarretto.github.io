Extracurricular Activities

Design Teams

I've harboured an interest in space for a long time, and finally convinced my parents to take me to the Kennedy Space Center in 2019, which easily became my favourite trip of all time. Thus, when I got to Waterloo, I had to join some space related teams. Currently I'm a member of Waterloo Orbital and Waterloo Rocketry.

On UW Orbital I do electrical/circuit related work. I learned how to properly source components, how to use Altium, and a lot about MOSFETs and load switch circuits which was what I worked on for the Electrical Power Systems team this past term.

I do more mechanical related work on UW Rocketry. Through contributing to the Rogallo Wing design, I gained practice doing stress tests on SolidWorks and expanded my knowledge of design principles.

The Debtless Students

When I recieved the $100,000 Schulich Leader Scholarship, I knew wanted to help other hardworking students feel the same gratitude and excitement that I was feeling. Thus, I joined an organization called the Debtless Students made up of Schulich Leaders, Loran Scholars, TD Scholarship winners, and more, whose goals aligned with my own. As a Scholarship Mentor, I help my mentees with essay and application content, help them find scholarships that suit them, and guide them through the university and scholarship application process. I also co-host webinars with fellow mentors on our team.

Engineering Ambassadors

I became an engineering ambassador this past term and had a lot of fun connecting with prospective students and helping them understand more about Waterloo Engineering. I remember attending open houses and asking upper years a bunch of questions last year, and was so happy to recieve well thought out answers from them - which made me want to be on the other side of those chats this year. I often meet with students over video calls to answer all their questions, and also ran a chat booth this year during the Waterloo open house.

Before entering university, my most prominent extracurricular activities included volunteering at the YMCA Employment Resource Centre, volunteering as a MiWay (Mississauga Transit) Ambassador, being the Deputy Prime Minister on Student Council, being a FIRST Robotics Team member, and being a member of the St. Vincent de Paul Society where we packaged and delivered food to the less fortunate.
